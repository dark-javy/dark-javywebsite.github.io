My Personality

I think the Enneagram personality type 9 (Peacemaker) best describes me. As a result of my disposition, I am easygoing, patient, and kind. I also try to avoid disagreement as much as possible in order to maintain a pleasant environment. To sustain my relationships with others, their demands are more likely to take precedence over mine. Struggling to establish myself has been a problem for me, and it also prevents me from taking action or making a decision. As a result of this, I get a sensation that a desire or desires of mine may not have been fulfilled. These traits I am working hard to overcome, however I still prefer to maintain a tranquil environment for my family and myself.
